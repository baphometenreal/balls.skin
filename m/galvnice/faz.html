faz

a combinatorial game

this is the RTF explaining the rules for the game faz. the name faz is pronounced /fa/ (phAzh), and as [] in its' original language. the objective of the game is to set your sphere on top of an upright cylinder. the color of the cylinder you put your sphere on does not matter. below is the full list of rules for the game.



how the pieces move

the cylinder

the cylinder is a very important piece. it can roll 1 or 2 squares along its' curve, and can also flip over on its' side onto the next square, putting it upright. in its' upright state, the next move it makes has to be to topple over. these three types of moves are illustrated below:

the wall

the wall is a very important piece. on any given turn, you can either move a piece or place a wall. the wall you place cannot move, and can only be jumped over by a sphere on a cube. if an arrangement of walls create an enclosed space anywhere on the board, the walls contributing to the closed space are removed, and you aren't able to place a wall directly after where one originally was. the three different ways that this can happen are illustrated below:
